What We Offer

Design

We design most parts in house using Fusion360 & LaserCAD

Manufacturing

We have six Haas Minimills that we use to manufacture our own parts alongside a laser cutter and multiple 3d printers

Projects

Our club offers a variety of projects including a Race GoKart, a Drift GoKart, and a 24 hours of Lemons car. We also have multiple RC cars students can work on

Competition

As a school we compete in a variety of competitions, but as a club we compete with GoKarts and the 24 Hours of Lemons
